Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What is your cancellation policy?

We offer free cancellation up to 7 days before your tour date. Cancellations within 7 days receive a 50% refund. No-shows are not refundable.

Do you provide transportation?

Yes, all our tours include transportation from designated pickup points in Kandy. For custom tours, we can arrange pickup from your hotel or preferred location.

What should I bring on a trek?

We provide a detailed packing list upon booking. Essentials include comfortable hiking shoes, water bottle, sun protection, and weather-appropriate clothing. We provide all camping equipment.

Are your tours suitable for beginners?

Yes! We offer tours for all fitness levels, from easy family-friendly walks to challenging mountain treks. Each tour is clearly marked with difficulty level.
